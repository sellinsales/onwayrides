Operations Control

Driver approval and document review.

Review pending driver applications, inspect secure uploads, approve or reject documents, and control when a driver can join the marketplace.

Who can use this page

Only local users with the role `admin` or `support` can access operational review actions.

Approval rule

Drivers should only be approved after required documents are reviewed. This page enforces the required minimum set before final approval.

Required documents for driver approval: profile photo, license, CNIC, and vehicle registration when a vehicle is assigned.

The operations page verifies your Firebase session first, then checks your local role in Laravel before enabling any approval actions.

Privacy Policy

How OnWay Rides handles personal data.

This policy explains what information OnWay Rides may collect, how it is used during beta and operational phases, and how users can request support or deletion.

Policy summary

Applies to riders, drivers, fleet partners, merchants, and business contacts using the OnWay Rides website, mobile app, and support channels.

Last updated: May 21, 2026 Support: support@onwayrides.com

Privacy Policy

OnWay Rides is a mobility platform that may support ride booking, delivery, rentals, airport travel, scheduled transport, driver onboarding, and related support operations. This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, stored, and disclosed.

1. Information we may collect

  • Account details such as name, phone number, email address, profile image, and login identifiers.
  • Trip and service data such as pickup and drop-off points, booking type, scheduled time, and support history.
  • Location-related information used to match rides, show trip progress, support dispatch, and improve service operations.
  • Driver, fleet, and vehicle documents used for verification and onboarding review.
  • Device, diagnostic, and usage information used to improve performance, detect abuse, and maintain reliability.
  • Payment and wallet-related metadata where applicable, but not full card data unless handled through a compliant payment provider.

2. How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To provide ride, delivery, rental, airport, and prebooking services.
  • To review driver and fleet onboarding documents.
  • To provide customer support, investigate complaints, and respond to disputes.
  • To monitor product quality during the beta phase, including usage limits such as maximum rides per day.
  • To improve product performance, trust, safety, fraud detection, and service operations.

3. Sharing of information

OnWay Rides may share limited information with drivers, riders, fleet partners, service providers, hosting providers, support teams, payment providers, and legal authorities where reasonably necessary to operate the platform, comply with the law, or protect users and the business.

4. Beta program notes

During the beta phase, OnWay Rides may collect operational and feedback information to evaluate product quality, improve service flow, review issues faster, and determine readiness for a wider rollout. Feature availability and usage limits may change while beta testing is active.

5. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the platform, provide support, comply with legal obligations, investigate abuse, maintain accounting records, and resolve disputes. Some records may be retained after account deletion where required by law or for legitimate safety, fraud, or audit purposes.

6. Security

OnWay Rides uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no system can be guaranteed fully secure. Users should keep their devices and account credentials protected.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You may request account correction or support by contacting support@onwayrides.com.
  • You may request account deletion using the process described on the Delete Account page.
  • You may stop using the app at any time, subject to ongoing obligations tied to bookings, disputes, or compliance review.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, support requests, or deletion enquiries, contact support@onwayrides.com.
